ABOUT

The Meals on Heels program was created to help deliver weekly essentials within the local community. Members of our community are in self-isolation or quarantine as we buckle down to address the COVID-19 pandemic. While many of us have access to the moral support and resources we need each day, some do not. Whether you are in need of your weekly shopping to be delivered or a smile to brighten your day Meals on Heels aims to deliver both.
